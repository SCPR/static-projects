November monthly water use by the Olivenhain Municipal Water District

Water consumption in the Olivenhain Municipal Water District increased 14.03 percent in November 2016 compared to November 2015. Overall, consumption in November has increased 1.87 percent over the past four years.
  • November 2013 440 million gallons
  • November 2014 497 million gallons
  • November 2015 380 million gallons
  • November 2016 434 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 73.0% of water consumption in the Olivenhain Municipal Water District is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 13.40 percent in November 2016 compared to November 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

Olivenhain Municipal Water District water use decreased by 25.61 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — but the agency failed to meet a 32 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate of gallons that places water agencies into nine tiers.
