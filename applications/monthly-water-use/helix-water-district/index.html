November monthly water use by the Helix Water District

Water consumption in the Helix Water District increased 2.95 percent in November 2016 compared to November 2015. Overall, consumption in November has decreased 7.40 percent over the past four years.
  • November 2013 836 million gallons
  • November 2014 840 million gallons
  • November 2015 752 million gallons
  • November 2016 774 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 80.0% of water consumption in the Helix Water District is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 2.42 percent in November 2016 compared to November 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

Helix Water District water use decreased by 21.55 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 20 percent reduction.
55.901
billion gallons consumed
over the same months in 2013.
43.857

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate of gallons that places water agencies into nine tiers.
