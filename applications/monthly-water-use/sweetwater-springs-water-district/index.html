June monthly water use by the Sweetwater Springs Water District

Water consumption in the Sweetwater Springs Water District increased 4.29 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 20.93 percent over the past five years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

<<<<<<< Updated upstream
About 83.0% of water consumption in the Sweetwater Springs Water District is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 3.89 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.
