August monthly water use by the City Of Shasta Lake

Water consumption in the City Of Shasta Lake increased 34.58 percent in August 2016 compared to August 2015. Overall, consumption in August has decreased 14.11 percent over the past three years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 79.0% of water consumption in the City Of Shasta Lake is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 39.89 percent in August 2016 compared to August 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Shasta Lake water use decreased by 37.88 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 28 percent reduction.
