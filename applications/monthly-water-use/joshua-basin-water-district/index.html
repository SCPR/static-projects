Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

<<<<<<< Updated upstream
About 91.848% of water consumption in the Joshua Basin Water District is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 6.68 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.
=======
