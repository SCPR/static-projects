August monthly water use by the City Of Hanford

Water consumption in the City Of Hanford decreased 12.20 percent in August 2016 compared to August 2015. Overall, consumption in August has decreased 17.19 percent over the past four years.
  • August 2013 541 million gallons
  • August 2014 399 million gallons
  • August 2015 510 million gallons
  • August 2016 448 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 89.0% of water consumption in the City Of Hanford is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 50.37 percent in August 2016 compared to August 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Hanford water use decreased by 17.21 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — but the agency failed to meet a 28 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate of gallons that places water agencies into nine tiers.
