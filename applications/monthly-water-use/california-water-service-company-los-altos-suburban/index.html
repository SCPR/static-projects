March monthly water use by the California Water Service Company Los Altos Suburban

Water consumption in the California Water Service Company Los Altos Suburban increased 1.13 percent in March 2017 compared to March 2016. Overall, consumption in March has decreased 43.01 percent over the past four years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 69.6% of water consumption in the California Water Service Company Los Altos Suburban is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer decreased 0.23 percent in March 2017 compared to March 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

California Water Service Company Los Altos Suburban water use decreased by 38.41 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 32 percent reduction.
