November monthly water use by the California Water Service Company Palos Verdes

Water consumption in the California Water Service Company Palos Verdes increased 0.79 percent in November 2016 compared to November 2015. Overall, consumption in November has decreased 17.53 percent over the past four years.
  • November 2013 517 million gallons
  • November 2014 503 million gallons
  • November 2015 423 million gallons
  • November 2016 426 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 84.0% of water consumption in the California Water Service Company Palos Verdes is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer decreased 2.27 percent in November 2016 compared to November 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

California Water Service Company Palos Verdes water use decreased by 27.96 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — but the agency failed to meet a 36 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide.
