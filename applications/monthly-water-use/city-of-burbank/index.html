December monthly water use by the City Of Burbank

Water consumption in the City Of Burbank decreased 0.92 percent in December 2017 compared to December 2017. Overall, consumption in December has decreased 21.28 percent over the past five years.
  • December 2013 459 million gallons
  • December 2014 353 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 75.0% of water consumption in the City Of Burbank is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 1.84 percent in December 2017 compared to December 2017, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Burbank water use decreased by 26.29 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 24 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate that placed water agencies into nine tiers.
