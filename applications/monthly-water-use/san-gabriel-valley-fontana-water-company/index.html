July monthly water use by the San Gabriel Valley Fontana Water Company

Water consumption in the San Gabriel Valley Fontana Water Company increased 8.80 percent in July 2017 compared to July 2016. Overall, consumption in July has decreased 13.95 percent over the past five years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 65.4% of water consumption in the San Gabriel Valley Fontana Water Company is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 0.95 percent in July 2017 compared to July 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

San Gabriel Valley Fontana Water Company water use decreased by 26.72 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — but the agency failed to meet a 28 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate that placed water agencies into nine tiers.
