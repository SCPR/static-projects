Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

<<<<<<< Updated upstream <<<<<<< Updated upstream
About 96.0% of water consumption in the Vaughn Water Company is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 6.32 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.
=======
The State Water Resources Control Board also collects a figure that represents the average daily water use of each residential customer for a given water agency.
