December monthly water use by the Bellflower-Somerset Mutual Water Company

Water consumption in the Bellflower-Somerset Mutual Water Company decreased 3.71 percent in December 2016 compared to December 2015. Overall, consumption in December has decreased 15.65 percent over the past four years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 86.0% of water consumption in the Bellflower-Somerset Mutual Water Company is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer decreased 3.71 percent in December 2016 compared to December 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

Bellflower-Somerset Mutual Water Company water use decreased by 18.82 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — but the agency failed to meet a 20 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate of gallons that places water agencies into nine tiers.
