June monthly water use by the City Of Loma Linda

Water consumption in the City Of Loma Linda increased 6.70 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 12.38 percent over the past five years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

<<<<<<< Updated upstream
About 68.6% of water consumption in the City Of Loma Linda is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 2.52 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.
=======
The State Water Resources Control Board also collects a figure that represents the average daily water use of each residential customer for a given water agency. Comparing Jan. 2016 — the latest monthly data available — to Dec. 2015, average daily per capita residential water use for the City Of Loma Linda decreased.
