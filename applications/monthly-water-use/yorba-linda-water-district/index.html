February monthly water use by the Yorba Linda Water District

Water consumption in the Yorba Linda Water District decreased 37.50 percent in February 2017 compared to February 2016. Overall, consumption in February has decreased 53.59 percent over the past four years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 74.0% of water consumption in the Yorba Linda Water District is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer decreased 38.33 percent in February 2017 compared to February 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

Yorba Linda Water District water use decreased by 36.73 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 36 percent reduction.
