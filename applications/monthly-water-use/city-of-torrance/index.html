May monthly water use by the City Of Torrance

Water consumption in the City Of Torrance increased 14.60 percent in May 2016 compared to May 2015. Overall, consumption in May has decreased 16.09 percent over the past three years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 58.0% of water consumption in the City Of Torrance is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 1.54 percent in May 2016 compared to May 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Torrance water use decreased by 19.96 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — but the agency failed to meet a 20 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate of gallons that places water agencies into nine tiers.
