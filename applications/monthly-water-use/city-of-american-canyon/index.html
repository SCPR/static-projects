July monthly water use by the City Of American Canyon

Water consumption in the City Of American Canyon increased 7.72 percent in July 2017 compared to July 2016. Overall, consumption in July has decreased 24.38 percent over the past five years.
  • July 2013 132 million gallons
  • July 2014 116 million gallons
  • July 2015 97 million gallons
  • July 2016 93 million gallons
  • July 2017 100 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 55.0% of water consumption in the City Of American Canyon is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 11.78 percent in July 2017 compared to July 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of American Canyon water use decreased by 27.14 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 20 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate that placed water agencies into nine tiers.
