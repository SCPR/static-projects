January monthly water use by the Sunnyslope County Water District

Water consumption in the Sunnyslope County Water District increased 14.91 percent in January 2017 compared to January 2016. Overall, consumption in January has decreased 7.94 percent over the past four years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 97.0% of water consumption in the Sunnyslope County Water District is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 11.26 percent in January 2017 compared to January 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

Sunnyslope County Water District water use decreased by 33.34 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 28 percent reduction.
