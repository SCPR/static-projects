June monthly water use by the City Of Huntington Beach

Water consumption in the City Of Huntington Beach increased 5.53 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 14.84 percent over the past five years.

  • June 2014 969 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 71.5% of water consumption in the City Of Huntington Beach is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 8.25 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Huntington Beach water use decreased by 20.44 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 20 percent reduction.
120.038
billion gallons consumed
over the same months in 2013.
95.500

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate that placed water agencies into nine tiers.
