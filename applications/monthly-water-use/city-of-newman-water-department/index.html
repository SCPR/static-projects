June monthly water use by the City Of Newman Water Department

Water consumption in the City Of Newman Water Department increased 19.01 percent in June 2016 compared to June 2015. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 14.99 percent over the past three years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 53.6% of water consumption in the City Of Newman Water Department is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 19.37 percent in June 2016 compared to June 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Newman Water Department water use decreased by 27.06 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 24 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide.
