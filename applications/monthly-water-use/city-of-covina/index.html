March monthly water use by the City Of Covina

Water consumption in the City Of Covina increased 4.59 percent in March 2017 compared to March 2016. Overall, consumption in March has decreased 27.39 percent over the past four years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 74.54% of water consumption in the City Of Covina is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 5.35 percent in March 2017 compared to March 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Covina water use decreased by 23.50 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — but the agency failed to meet a 28 percent reduction.
