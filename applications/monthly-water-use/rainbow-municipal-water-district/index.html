June monthly water use by the Rainbow Municipal Water District

Water consumption in the Rainbow Municipal Water District increased 1.14 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 12.22 percent over the past five years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

<<<<<<< Updated upstream <<<<<<< Updated upstream
About 24.5% of water consumption in the Rainbow Municipal Water District is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 12.23 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.
=======
The State Water Resources Control Board also collects a figure that represents the average daily water use of each residential customer for a given water agency. Comparing Jan. 2016 — the latest monthly data available — to Dec. 2015, average daily per capita residential water use for the Rainbow Municipal Water District decreased.
