June monthly water use by the City Of Delano

Water consumption in the City Of Delano increased 9.17 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 12.65 percent over the past five years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

<<<<<<< Updated upstream
About 48.0% of water consumption in the City Of Delano is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer decreased 14.92 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.
=======
The State Water Resources Control Board also collects a figure that represents the average daily water use of each residential customer for a given water agency.
