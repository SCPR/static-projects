July monthly water use by the City Of Sacramento

Water consumption in the City Of Sacramento increased 7.22 percent in July 2017 compared to July 2016. Overall, consumption in July has decreased 19.66 percent over the past five years.
  • July 2013 4766 million gallons
  • July 2014 3709 million gallons
  • July 2015 3099 million gallons
  • July 2016 3571 million gallons
  • July 2017 3829 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 65.0% of water consumption in the City Of Sacramento is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 4.03 percent in July 2017 compared to July 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Sacramento water use decreased by 29.29 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 28 percent reduction.

over the same months in 2013.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate that placed water agencies into nine tiers.
