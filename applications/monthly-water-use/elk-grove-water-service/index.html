November monthly water use by the Elk Grove Water Service

Water consumption in the Elk Grove Water Service decreased 9.71 percent in November 2016 compared to November 2015. Overall, consumption in November has decreased 43.94 percent over the past four years.
  • November 2013 179 million gallons
  • November 2014 131 million gallons
  • November 2015 111 million gallons
  • November 2016 100 million gallons

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 79.0% of water consumption in the Elk Grove Water Service is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer decreased 9.65 percent in November 2016 compared to November 2015, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

Elk Grove Water Service water use decreased by 35.42 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 28 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate of gallons that places water agencies into nine tiers.
