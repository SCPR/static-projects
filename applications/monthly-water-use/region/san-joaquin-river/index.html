About the data: These charts show the daily per capita residential water use rate in California as calculated by the State Water Resources Control Board. The charts cover the December 2017 reporting period, and are compared to the previous month unless specified. Since the per capita measure (which reflects gallons of water used per resident per day) does not take into account rainfall, temperature, humidity and population density, the State Water Resources Control Board discourages comparisons between water suppliers.

San Joaquin River

Daily per capita residential water use decreased in December 2017 to an average of 64.67 gallons for the 25 agencies in the San Joaquin River hydrologic region. That number is compared to the November 2017 daily per capita residential water use of 66.38.
