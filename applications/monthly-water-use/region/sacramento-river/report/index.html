About the data: These charts show the daily per capita residential water use rate in California as calculated by the State Water Resources Control Board. The charts cover the Aug. 2015 reporting period, and are compared to the previous month unless specified. Since the per capita measure (which reflects gallons of water used per resident per day) does not take into account rainfall, temperature, humidity and population density, the State Water Resources Control Board discourages comparisons between water suppliers.

Sacramento River

Daily per capita residential water use decreased in Aug. 2015 to an average of 150.25 gallons for the 43 agencies in the Sacramento River hydrologic region. That number is compared to the July 2015 daily per capita residential water use of 151.82.
