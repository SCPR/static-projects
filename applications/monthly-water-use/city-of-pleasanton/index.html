June monthly water use by the City Of Pleasanton

Water consumption in the City Of Pleasanton increased 11.54 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 25.32 percent over the past five years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

<<<<<<< Updated upstream <<<<<<< Updated upstream
About 59.2% of water consumption in the City Of Pleasanton is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer decreased 4.40 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.
=======
The State Water Resources Control Board also collects a figure that represents the average daily water use of each residential customer for a given water agency.
