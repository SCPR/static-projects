January monthly water use by the California Water Service Company Mid Peninsula

Water consumption in the California Water Service Company Mid Peninsula increased 3.24 percent in January 2017 compared to January 2016. Overall, consumption in January has decreased 10.52 percent over the past four years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 74.4% of water consumption in the California Water Service Company Mid Peninsula is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 3.44 percent in January 2017 compared to January 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

California Water Service Company Mid Peninsula water use decreased by 24.10 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 16 percent reduction.
