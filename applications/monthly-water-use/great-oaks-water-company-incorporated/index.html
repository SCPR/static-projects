June monthly water use by the Great Oaks Water Company Incorporated

Water consumption in the Great Oaks Water Company Incorporated increased 16.31 percent in June 2016 compared to June 2015. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 26.14 percent over the past three years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

Great Oaks Water Company Incorporated water use decreased by 32.99 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — achieving a 20 percent reduction.
