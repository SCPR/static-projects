June monthly water use by the Golden State Water Company Claremont

Water consumption in the Golden State Water Company Claremont increased 12.77 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016. Overall, consumption in June has decreased 24.30 percent over the past five years.

About 63.6% of water consumption in the Golden State Water Company Claremont is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer increased 12.62 percent in June 2017 compared to June 2016, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.
