December monthly water use by the City Of Indio

Water consumption in the City Of Indio decreased 2.02 percent in December 2017 compared to December 2017. Overall, consumption in December has decreased 20.78 percent over the past five years.

Average daily water consumption by each residential customer

About 72.0% of water consumption in the City Of Indio is residential. The average daily gallons used by each residential customer decreased 1.97 percent in December 2017 compared to December 2017, according to the State Water Resources Control Board.

Progress toward mandatory water reduction*

City Of Indio water use decreased by 22.81 percent between June 2015 and February 2016 — the nine months of the initial statewide conservation mandate — but the agency failed to meet a 32 percent reduction.

*: Approved by the State Water Resources Control Board in 2015, the reduction target was meant to achieve a 25 percent reduction in water use statewide. It is tied to an average July through Sept. 2014 daily per capita residential water use rate that placed water agencies into nine tiers.
