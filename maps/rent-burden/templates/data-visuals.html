About the data:

Renter data for Zip Code Tabulation Areas in Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside, San Bernardino and Ventura counties via Census Reporter and based on American Community Survey 2012 5-year estimates. ZCTAs for which the coefficient variable for the estimated number of renters is more than 10 percent are not displayed. Number of renters is based on occupied housing units from ACS Table B25003. Median monthly rent is based on renter-occupied housing units paying cash rent from ACS Table B25064. Median annual income is based on occupied housing units from the past 12 months and displayed in 2012 inflation-adjusted dollars from ACS Table B25119. Median rent burden is based on renter-occupied housing units paying cash rent from the past 12 months from ACS Table B25071. Household rent burden as a percentage of its income is based on renter-occupied housing units from the past 12 months from ACS Table B25070. GIS data for Zip Code Tabulation Areas via the L.A. Times Mapping L.A. Boundaries API.
