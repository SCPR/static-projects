The who, what, where & when on downtown LA Development

The downtown Los Angeles skyline continues to evolve; lately, at a seemingly breakneck speed. New hotels, lofts, bars, restaurants, parks and transit routes are popping up from Bunker Hill to the Arts District. To help keep up, this map will track projects in the city's core. And we will continue to update downtown development projects from planning to opening.
