Trips by station in L.A.'s bike share program

Produced by Aaron Mendelson | Published April 20, 2017

Metro's bike share network logged more than 130,000 trips in its first nine months. Below, you can explore the number of trips that started and ended at each of the network's Downtown Los Angeles stations.

Source: Metro data through Q1 of 2017. The map does not include a small number of trips coded as being used by staff or that did not have a recorded start or end station. Built using leaflet.js, underscore.js & Bootstrap.
