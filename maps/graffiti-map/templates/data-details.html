Map: A month in Los Angeles graffiti

Produced by Aaron Mendelson | Published August 17, 2015

The City of Los Angeles keeps a record of every sighting of graffiti that citizens and its roving bands of contractors report. The city has recorded more than two million reports of graffiti since 2010. Using this data from the Office of Community Beautificaiton, KPCC mapped a single month in graffiti: January 2015. The bigger the circle, the more square feet OCB and its contractors reported cleaning up.

Source: Los Angeles Office of Community Beautification. Built using underscore.js, backbone.js, Stamen, Time Manager & Bootstrap.
