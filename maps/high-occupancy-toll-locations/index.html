Metro ExpressLanes on the 110 and 10 freeways

Starting Nov. 10 motorists traveling the 110 freeway between the Artesia Transit Center to Adams Boulevard, and the 110 freeway from the 605 to Alameda Street will have the option of paying congestion pricing to travel in High Occupancy Toll lanes.
