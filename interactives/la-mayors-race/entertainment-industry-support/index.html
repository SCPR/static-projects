Using this interactive: The Los Angeles City Ethics Commission provides data to the public about campaign contributors, including their employers and occupations. Those employers and occupation descriptions were used to determine which donations came from the entertainment industry. This visual shows the 10 largest contributing groups in the entertainment industry. Hover over a section to see how much money an entertainment sector contributed to a particular candidate. Click on a section to zoom in to further explore campaign contributions. Right-click on the section to zoom out.
