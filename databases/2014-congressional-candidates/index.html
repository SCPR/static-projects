Candidates for Southern California congressional seats

Produced by Chris Keller & Kitty Felde | Updated March 13, 2014

Nominations for California's June 3 primary closed Tuesday. The field of candidates vying for congressional seats in Southern California districts is now clear.

Below is a list of those who filed the paperwork to run for Congressional districts in Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside and San Bernardino counties in addition to their party affiliation and a brief bio – where applicable.

Six Southern California incumbents chose not to run in 2014, including veterans George Miller and Henry Waxman. Combine that with the election of 14 brand new representatives in the 2012 election and suddenly about 38 percent of the state's delegation will have less than three years of experience on the Hill by the time they take office.

One newcomer wil lbe one of the more than 22 candidates that have filed to run for Waxman's 33rd District Seat.

California allows anyone from any party to vote for any candidate in the June 3rd primary, with the top two finishers facing off in November.
