Candidates for Southern California congressional seats

Produced by Kitty Felde & Chris Keller | Updated Feb. 24, 2014

Candidates for Californias Congressional seats have until March 7 to circulate nominating papers and file a declaration of candidacy.

Below is a list of those who have declared their intent to run for Congressional seats in Southern California districts.

Source(s): KPCC staff reports. Built using Table Stacker, a Django application that publishes spreadsheets, handlebars.js & Bootstrap.
