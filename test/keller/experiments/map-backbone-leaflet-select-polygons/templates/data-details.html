LA County's 3rd Supervisor District

Produced by Chris Keller | Updated June 20, 2014

After finishing as the top-two vote getters from the June 3 primary, former state lawmaker Sheila Kuehl and former Santa Monica Mayor Bobby Shriver are headed for a November election to determine who will represent the 3rd District on the LA County Board of Supervisors.
