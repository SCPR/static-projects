We can't bring you all of their public statements, but thanks to CapitolWords, a Sunlight Foundation project that culls speeches from the Congressional Record and makes them searchable by topic, state and legislator, we're able to shed some light on the state delegation's thoughts leading up to and since the federal government closed its doors.

Source(s): Congressional Record since Aug. 1, 2013 as indexed each day by CapitolWords. Built using CapitolWords, underscore.js, backbone.js, jQuery Highlight Plugin & Bootstrap.
