Capital Bonds...

Capital appreciation bonds: Unlike typical bonds, where a school district is required to make immediate and regular payments, CABs allow districts to defer payments well into the future by which time lots of interest has accrued.

Hundreds of California school and community college districts have financed construction projects with capital appreciation bonds that defer repayment for years.

Ultimately the district will owe much more than it borrowed.
