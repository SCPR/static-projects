California Drought

Am I under any water restrictions?

Produced by Molly Peterson & Chris Keller | Published Feb. 20, 2013

Last month Gov. Jerry Brown declared a drought emergency in California and asked state residents to voluntarily cut water usage by 20 percent.

In Northern California, some counties and cities have begun mandatory conservation measures for residents. But in Southern California, most local water districts have only asked for voluntary measures.

We gathered data on water restrictions from city and water agency websites served by the Metropolitan Water District. Below, you can find restrictions in your municipality, if any. You can also find tips to conserve water.
