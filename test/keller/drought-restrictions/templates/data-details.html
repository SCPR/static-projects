California Drought

Am I under any water restrictions?

Produced by Molly Peterson & Chris Keller | Published Feb. 13, 2013

Last month Gov. Jerry Brown declared a drought emergency in California and asked state residents to voluntarily cut water usage by 20 percent.

In Northern California, some counties and cities have begun mandatory conservation measures for residents. But in Southern California, most local water districts have only asked for voluntary measures ala the governor's.

Below, you can find many Southern California municipalities, restrictions in place -- if any -- gathered from city and water district websites and some helpful tips to conserve water.
