Garcetti's behested payments

Produced by Aaron Mendelson | Published August 7, 2017

While Los Angeles has tight controls on the amount of money citizens can donate to campaigns, there's no limit on the amounts of cash people, foundations, non-profits and corporations can donate to government and charities at a politician's behest.

Since Los Angeles Mayor Eric Garcetti was elected in 2013, he has reported nearly $30 million in payments at his behest. The majority of those payments have gone to The Mayor's Fund For Los Angeles, a non-profit dedicated to promoting civic causes including reducing homelessness, workforce development and gang reduction.
