Does your bus stay on time?

Produced by Aaron Mendelson | Published Feb. 19, 2015

Data covering tens of millions of stops by Metro buses shows that some bus lines are chronically late, while others regularly pull away from stops on time. The gif below shows the lines that are late most often (those are in orange) and the lines that show up on time most often (in green). Only lines with more than 250,000 stops recorded in Metro's data over the last five years are displayed here.

Source(s): Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority.
