#WhoMailedIt: Tracking political mailers

Produced by Aaron Mendelson | Published October 15, 2016

Even with all the new technology at their fingertips, candidates and the groups that support them continue to count on the U.S. Postal Service as a conduit to voters, touting their message or slamming the competition on glossy mailers. Mailers are highly targeted, and state government doesn't comprehensively track them, so it can be hard to see what kinds of claims are floating around out there. Here are a few collected through KPCC's #WhoMailedIt project.

See something in your mailbox that we don't have here? Tweet it with the hashtag #WhoMailedIt, or email us at whomailedit@scpr.org.
