Blue and green campaign lit landing in mailboxes from Los Feliz to Monrovia touts Anthony Portantino as a tough progressive. But another mailer looks back on his record as a member of the Assembly, claiming he raised health care premiums and cut school funding.

If you read the fine print, you'll see that neither mailer was sent by Portantino or his opponent, Mike Antonovich. Instead, these were sent by the Democratic and Republican parties of California.
