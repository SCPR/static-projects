25th Senate District

Blue and green campaign literature is landing in mailboxes from Tujunga to Upland touts Anthony Portantino as a tough progressive. But another mailer looks back on his record as a member of the Assembly, claiming he raised health care premiums and cut school funding. If you read the fine print, you'll see that neither mailer was sent by Portantino or his opponent, Mike Antonovich. Instead, these were sent by the Democratic and Republican parties of California.
