A year in LADWP claims payouts

Produced by Aaron Mendelson | Published July 25, 2015

When the Los Angeles Department of Water and Power screws up, they have to pay up. If a water main bursts and floods your car, power surge fries your TV, or vehicle runs over a family member, the LADWP could be on the hook for damages to you or your insurance comapny.

The department paid out 624 claims in the year between May 2014 and April 2015. It breaks down each claim by types. Here are the types that cost the department more than a $100,000 last year. TK here is where we are keeping the data and a link to it.
